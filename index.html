Hello, I'm Rishi

As an industrial designer, I believe that great design is the perfect intersection of aesthetic appeal, functional excellence, and meaningful user experience. My approach combines deep user research, creative problem-solving, and technical expertise to create products that truly resonate with people.

With a background in mechanical engineering and design thinking, I bring both technical feasibility and creative vision to every project. I'm passionate about sustainable design practices and creating products that have a positive impact on both users and the environment.

When I'm not designing, you can find me sketching new ideas, exploring emerging technologies, or analyzing the design details of everyday objects around me.
